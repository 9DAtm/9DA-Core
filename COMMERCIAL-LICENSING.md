\## Licensing Structure



\### Non-Exclusive



All licenses are non-exclusive. Multiple organizations may implement 9DA-compliant systems.



\### Independent Licenses



Trademark and tooling licenses are independent:

\- You may license trademark without tooling

\- You may license tooling without trademark (for internal use)



\### Case-by-Case Negotiation



All terms negotiated individually:

\- Scope of use

\- Geographic restrictions (if any)

\- License duration

\- Fee structure

\- Support level



\### No Tying



Trademark licensing does NOT require:

\- Purchase of other licenses

\- Use of specific tooling

\- Exclusive arrangements



---



\## License Fees



\### No Published Pricing



License fees are determined through negotiation based on:

\- Implementation scope

\- Organization size

\- Commercial vs. non-commercial use

\- Support requirements



\### No Standard Rate Card



Each license is priced individually. Public pricing is not available.



\### Open Source Considerations



Favorable terms may be available for:

\- Open source implementations

\- Educational institutions

\- Non-profit organizations

\- Research projects



---



\## Compliance Requirements



\### Objective Verification



Trademark licensing requires:

\- Cryptographic proof of compliance

\- Schema validation passing

\- Zero architectural violations

\- Ethics compliance verified



\### Continuous Compliance



Licenses require ongoing compliance:

\- Regular re-verification

\- Immediate disclosure of violations

\- Compliance drift monitoring



\### Re-Verification on Modification



Any structural change requires:

\- Updated compliance proof

\- Possible license review

\- Re-certification if breaking changes



---



\## License Termination



\### Automatic Termination



Licenses terminate automatically on:

\- Specification sunset (if defined)

\- Compliance failure

\- Breach of license terms



\### Revocation



Licenses may be revoked for:

\- Compliance violations

\- Trademark misuse

\- Breach of ethics constraints

\- Fraudulent compliance claims



See \[docs/ESCALATION.md](docs/ESCALATION.md) for violation procedures.

\## Fork Freedom



\### Forks Permitted



You may fork the 9DA specification and create derivative governance frameworks.



\### Trademark Restrictions



Forks may NOT:

\- Use the "9DA" trademark

\- Claim compliance with 9DA

\- Use 9DA badges or certifications



Forks must:

\- Use distinct names

\- Clearly indicate derivation

\- Remove all 9DA branding
